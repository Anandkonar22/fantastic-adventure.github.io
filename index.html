You are incredibly important to me, and I value our relationship more than anything. I want us to move past this and grow stronger together. I am dedicated to making things right and proving to you that my love and commitment are unwavering.

Thank you for taking the time to read this. I hope you can find it in your heart to forgive me. I am here for you, ready to listen, and ready to work on rebuilding our trust and love.

With all my love and deepest apologies,
Anand
